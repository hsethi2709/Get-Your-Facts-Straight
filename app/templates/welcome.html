Welcome to Get Fact Check Experiment!

We have built a fact checking chrome extension which allows you to highlight any sentence on the internet to check it's authenticity.

The extension responds back with evidences along with the label which either 'Supports' or 'Refutes' the claim you have highlighted.

The purpose of the experiment is to find out the most effective way of presenting evidences in a tool like this to curb the menance of misinformation online.

In the next page, there will be a practise round to make you familiar with the extension.
